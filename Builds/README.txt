Game Title

Welcome to Night Watch!

Prepare yourself for a challenging Hard Mode experience — every decision matters, and danger is everywhere.

Controls

Move: W, A, S, D

Jump: Spacebar

Shoot: Left Mouse Click

Interact: E

Activate Flashlight: F

Pause / Menu: Tab

Gameplay Tips

Shoot the rats to stun them before they attack.

Find the switch to activate the gates and escape the area.

Avoid water — touching it causes instant death and resets you to the start.

Collect health and ammo crates to respawn resources and stay alive.

Use your flashlight wisely to spot hazards and enemies.

Objective

Survive the environment, defeat threats, and unlock the gates by finding the switch. Manage your health and ammo carefully — you’ll need them to succeed.

Warning

This is Hard Mode; death resets your progress.

Water is lethal.

Enemy rats will track you down if you get too close.

Good luck! Stay alert, conserve resources, and escape alive!
